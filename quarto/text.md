Netto strongly denied these allegations, stating that Brazil had not received any such information from Israel. He further criticized Israel for making public statements without verifying the facts. The Brazilian government has requested an explanation from Israel and expects a formal apology. The incident has strained the diplomatic relations between the two countries. Hezbollah, a Lebanese militant group, has been designated as a terrorist organization by several countries, including the United States and Israel. Brazil has been cooperating with international partners to combat terrorism and maintain national security.
