# Citi's 2023 Revenue Expected to be at Lower End of Forecast, CFO Says

## Professional English
Citigroup Inc's Chief Financial Officer (CFO) announced that the company's revenue for 2023 is expected to be at the lower end of the forecast. This news comes as a result of the ongoing challenges faced by the global financial institution. The CFO stated that the revenue decline is primarily due to the impact of the pandemic on the economy and the uncertainties surrounding the recovery. Despite this setback, Citigroup remains committed to its long-term growth strategy and is focused on optimizing its operations and improving efficiency. The company aims to navigate through the current market conditions and emerge stronger in the future.

The announcement by Citigroup's CFO reflects the broader challenges faced by the banking industry in the aftermath of the pandemic. Many financial institutions have experienced a decline in revenue as a result of reduced economic activity and increased market volatility. The uncertain economic outlook has made it difficult for banks to accurately forecast their earnings. However, Citigroup's commitment to its growth strategy indicates its determination to overcome these challenges and adapt to the changing market conditions.
