# "Third-Party Candidates Pose Threat to Biden in 2024, Says WSJ"

## Professional English
According to a recent article by The Wall Street Journal (WSJ), third-party candidates could potentially pose a threat to President Joe Biden's re-election bid in 2024. The article highlights the historical impact of third-party candidates on past elections and suggests that their presence in the upcoming election could divide the Democratic vote. The WSJ points out that third-party candidates often attract voters who are dissatisfied with the two major parties, and their participation could potentially siphon off votes that would have otherwise gone to Biden. The article also discusses the potential influence of prominent figures like former President Donald Trump, who might choose to run as a third-party candidate. While it remains uncertain whether third-party candidates will have a significant impact on the 2024 election, the WSJ suggests that their presence could complicate Biden's path to re-election.
