He further criticized the Israeli government for making such statements without providing evidence. The Brazilian government has demanded a public apology from Israel for the false accusations. The incident has strained the diplomatic relations between the two countries, as Brazil considers Hezbollah a terrorist organization and has been actively working to combat its activities within its borders. The Brazilian government has also expressed concerns about the potential impact of these false accusations on its international reputation. The Israeli government has not yet responded to the Brazilian demands for an apology.
