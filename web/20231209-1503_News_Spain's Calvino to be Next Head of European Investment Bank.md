# Spain's Calvino to be Next Head of European Investment Bank

## Professional English
Spain's Economy Minister, Nadia Calvino, has been nominated to become the next President of the European Investment Bank (EIB). If approved, Calvino will be the first woman to hold this position. The EIB is the European Union's lending institution, providing financial support for projects that promote sustainable growth and job creation. Calvino's nomination comes at a crucial time as the EU seeks to recover from the economic impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. Her experience in economic policy and international finance makes her a strong candidate for the role. Calvino has previously served as the Director-General for Budget at the European Commission and has played a key role in shaping Spain's economic recovery plan. Her appointment as the head of the EIB would further strengthen Spain's influence in European financial institutions.
