1  Gaza Hospitals Under Attack by Israeli Forces - Reuters

1.1 Professional English

Gaza Officials Say Hospitals Come Under New Israeli Attacks Gaza hospitals have reportedly come under new attacks by Israeli forces, according to officials in the region. The attacks have raised concerns about the already strained healthcare system in Gaza, which has been grappling with a surge in COVID-19 cases. The officials have accused Israel of deliberately targeting medical facilities, including a hospital that was hit by an airstrike. The Israeli military has stated that it only targets sites used by Hamas, the militant group that controls Gaza. However, the United Nations has called for an immediate halt to the attacks on hospitals and other civilian infrastructure. The escalating violence in the region has resulted in a growing number of casualties, including many civilians. The international community has expressed deep concern over the situation and has called for a peaceful resolution to the conflict.
