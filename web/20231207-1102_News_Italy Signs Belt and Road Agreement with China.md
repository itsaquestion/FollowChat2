# Italy Signs Belt and Road Agreement with China

## Professional English
Italy has signed an agreement with China to join the Belt and Road Initiative (BRI), becoming the first G7 country to do so. The deal was signed during Chinese President Xi Jinping's visit to Rome. The Belt and Road Initiative is a massive infrastructure project aimed at connecting Asia, Europe, and Africa through a network of roads, railways, ports, and other infrastructure. Italy's decision to join the initiative has raised concerns among its European Union partners and the United States, who worry about China's growing influence in Europe. However, Italian officials argue that the agreement will bring economic benefits to the country, including increased trade and investment. Critics, on the other hand, raise concerns about the lack of transparency and potential debt risks associated with the BRI projects.
