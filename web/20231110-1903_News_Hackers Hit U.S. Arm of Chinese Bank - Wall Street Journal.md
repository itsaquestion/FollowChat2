# Hackers Hit U.S. Arm of Chinese Bank - Wall Street Journal

## Professional English
In a recent cyber attack, hackers targeted the U.S. division of a major Chinese bank, according to sources. The attack, which occurred last week, resulted in the theft of sensitive customer data. The bank has not disclosed the number of affected customers or the extent of the data breach. However, experts believe that the hackers gained access to personal and financial information, including account numbers and social security numbers. The bank is working closely with law enforcement agencies to investigate the incident and enhance its cybersecurity measures. This attack highlights the growing threat of cybercrime and the need for organizations to strengthen their defenses against such attacks. Cybersecurity experts recommend that individuals and businesses regularly update their security software, use strong and unique passwords, and be cautious of suspicious emails or links. The investigation into this cyber attack is ongoing, and further details are expected to emerge in the coming weeks.
