# "Celsius Network Cleared to Exit Bankruptcy, Reuters Reports"

## Professional English
Celsius Network, a cryptocurrency lending platform, has been given approval to exit bankruptcy, according to a report by Reuters. The company had filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in August 2023 after facing financial difficulties. However, a U.S. bankruptcy judge has now cleared Celsius Network to emerge from bankruptcy and continue its operations. The judge's decision was based on the company's successful restructuring plan, which involved reducing its debt and securing new funding. Celsius Network, founded in 2017, offers interest-earning accounts and loans backed by cryptocurrencies. The company has gained popularity in the crypto industry, attracting over 1 million users and managing more than $20 billion in assets. With its exit from bankruptcy, Celsius Network aims to regain stability and further expand its services in the growing cryptocurrency market.
