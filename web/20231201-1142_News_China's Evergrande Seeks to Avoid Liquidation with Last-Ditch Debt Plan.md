# China's Evergrande Seeks to Avoid Liquidation with Last-Ditch Debt Plan

## Professional English
China's troubled property giant, Evergrande, is making a last-ditch effort to avoid liquidation by proposing a debt restructuring plan. The company, which is facing a massive debt crisis, has been struggling to repay its creditors and has been on the brink of collapse. According to sources, Evergrande is seeking to extend the maturity of its debt and reduce interest payments in order to ease its financial burden. The proposed plan is currently being reviewed by the company's creditors, and its success will determine the fate of Evergrande and its thousands of employees.
